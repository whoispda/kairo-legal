Common questions

Where is my data? On your iPhone. Kairo has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics — see the privacy policy.

How do I manage or cancel Kairo Labs? Subscriptions are handled by Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions, or the Manage Subscription link inside the app's Profile tab. Restore Purchases lives on the paywall and in Profile.

How do I export or delete my data? Profile → Export My Data gives you CSV files. Profile → reset controls erase everything on the device; deleting the app does too.

Is Kairo medical advice? No. Kairo is wellness education and self-tracking. If you're pregnant, under 18, or managing a health condition, talk to a professional before fasting.
