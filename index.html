Questions, answered plainly

The things people actually ask

Do I need an Apple Watch, WHOOP, or Oura?

No. If you wear one, keep its app syncing to Apple Health and Kairo reads those signals automatically — no extra login. If you don't, Kairo still knows how you slept, because you told it — a five-second log tunes today's fast.

What happens to my data?

It stays on your phone. There's no account, no server of ours, no analytics trackers. Optional iCloud sync uses your private iCloud. You can export or erase everything from inside the app. Details in the privacy policy.

Is the AI coach sending my conversations somewhere?

No. Coach Kai runs on Apple's on-device model — the conversation happens on your iPhone's own chip and never leaves it. That's also why he's unlimited rather than metered.

What's actually free?

The whole core loop: the live timer with all ten stages, streaks, history, awards, hydration, reminders, sleep logging, and the learning track. Kairo Labs adds the intelligence — readiness, adaptive windows, Coach Kai, programs, and the full cited library.

Is fasting right for me?

Not always — and Kairo won't pretend otherwise. If you're pregnant, have a history of disordered eating, or manage a medical condition, talk to a professional first. Kairo provides general wellness education, not medical advice.

When can I get it?

Kairo is finishing beta and coming soon to the App Store for iPhone. Drop a line and you'll hear the day it's live.
