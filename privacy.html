Kairo Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2026

Kairo is a fasting tracker for iPhone, made by an independent developer. The

short version: Kairo does not collect your data. There are no accounts, no

ads, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services in the app.

Data stored on your device

Everything you record in Kairo — fasting sessions and history, weight and

body-fat logs, hydration entries, reminders, plans, and settings — is stored

locally on your iPhone. It is never transmitted to us. We have no servers and

no way to see it.

Deleting the app deletes this data.

Apple Health (optional)

Apple Health is optional and off by default. If you grant permission:

  • Kairo writes the weight and body-fat entries you log, and Focus Mode

sessions as mindful minutes, into the Apple Health app on your device.

  • Kairo reads sleep, heart-rate variability, resting heart rate, and

active energy to compute your readiness and recovery insights — entirely on

your device.

Health data never leaves your iPhone, is never used for marketing, and is

never shared with anyone. You can revoke these permissions at any time in the

iPhone Settings app, and you control the data in Apple Health directly.

iCloud sync (if offered in your version)

If your version of Kairo offers iCloud sync and you enable it, your fasting

history and body logs are stored in your personal, private iCloud database,

protected by your Apple Account. That data belongs to you; we cannot access,

read, or share it. Apple's iCloud terms and privacy protections apply.

Coach Insights

Coach Insights are generated on your device, using Apple's on-device

intelligence when available. Your fasting data is not sent to us or to any

third party to produce them.

Purchases

Kairo Labs subscriptions and the Lifetime purchase are processed by Apple through your Apple Account.

We never see your payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs payment

processing. Kairo checks your subscription status on-device through Apple's

StoreKit framework.

Notifications

Reminders (fast completion, daily check-in, plan reminders) are local

notifications scheduled on your device. They do not involve any server.

No tracking

Kairo contains no advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no trackers, and it does

not access the advertising identifier. The App Store privacy label "Data Not

Collected" applies.

Children

Kairo is not directed at children and is intended for adults choosing to track

fasting.

Health disclaimer

Kairo provides general wellness information only and is not a medical device.

It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult your

physician before beginning any fasting practice.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of Kairo changes how data is handled, this policy will be

updated at this address before that version ships, with a new effective date.
