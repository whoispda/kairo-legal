Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

The short version: Kairo doesn't collect your data. No accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services. Your fasting story lives on your iPhone — we couldn't read it even if we wanted to. The rest of this page just spells that out.

Everything stays on your device

Everything you record in Kairo — fasting sessions and history, weight and body-fat logs, journal check-ins, sleep logs, hydration, workouts you log, plans, reminders, and settings — is stored locally on your iPhone. It is never transmitted to us. We have no servers and no way to see it.

Deleting the app deletes this data.

Apple Health — optional, and off until you say so

Kairo only touches Apple Health if you grant permission, and you choose exactly what to share. If you do:

  • Kairo writes the weight and body-fat entries you log, your Focus Mode sessions as mindful minutes, and the workouts you log in Kairo (with their active energy and distance) into Apple Health on your device.
  • Kairo reads sleep, heart-rate variability, resting heart rate, active energy, and your workouts (with heart rate and walking, cycling, and swimming distances) to understand your recovery and readiness — entirely on your device.

Health data never leaves your iPhone, is never used for marketing, and is never shared with anyone — and it is never uploaded to iCloud. You can revoke these permissions any time in the iPhone Settings app, and you control the data in Apple Health directly.

iCloud sync — your data, your iCloud

iCloud sync is off by default. If you turn it on, your fasting history, body logs, journal check-ins, and the workouts you logged by hand are stored in your personal, private iCloud database, protected by your Apple Account. That data belongs to you; we cannot access, read, or share it. Workouts read from Apple Health never sync — they stay on each device. Apple's iCloud terms and privacy protections apply.

Coach Kai thinks on your phone

Kai's insights are generated on your device, using Apple's on-device intelligence when available. Your fasting data is never sent to us — or to any third party — to produce them.

Purchases

Kairo Labs subscriptions and the Lifetime purchase are processed by Apple through your Apple Account. We never see your payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs payment processing; Kairo checks your subscription status on-device through Apple's StoreKit framework.

Notifications

Reminders — fast completion, daily check-in, plan reminders — are local notifications scheduled on your device. No server is involved.

You hold the keys

  • Export: Profile → Export My Data gives you your records as CSV files, any time.
  • Retention: Keep Raw Logs lets you auto-delete old check-in moments and sleep logs on your schedule.
  • Erase: Profile's reset controls permanently erase everything on the device — and deleting the app does too.
  • Discretion: Kairo hides your numbers in the iPhone app switcher, so your progress stays yours.

No tracking. Full stop.

Kairo contains no advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no trackers, and it does not access the advertising identifier. The App Store privacy label "Data Not Collected" applies.

Children

Kairo is not directed at children and is intended for adults choosing to track fasting.

Health disclaimer

Kairo provides general wellness information only and is not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult your physician before beginning any fasting practice.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of Kairo changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated at this address before that version ships, with a new effective date.
